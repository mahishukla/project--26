Crumble the butter, flour and ground almonds through your fingers till you get a crumbly mixture. Then mix the egg yolk with the caster sugar. Add the egg mixture in small amounts to the crumbly flour mixture while mixing with a stand mixer with a dough hook or a large spatula. Knead the dough for 2 minutes and then wrap the bowl with plastic wrap and put it in the fridge for about 10 minutes. Remove the dough from the fridge and roll it on a floured surface. Transfer it to a 8 inch tart pan. Mould the dough according to the tart pan. Place in the fridge for 30-40 minutes. Remove out of the fridge before making the jam. Frangipane: Beat the butter, caster sugar and lemon rind until a fluffy paste forms. Mix in the two eggs. Finally add all the dry ingredients until a creamy paste forms. Wrap it with plastic wrap and let it set in the fridge for 20 minutes. Take out of the fridge before making the jam.
